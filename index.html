Hi!

A creative mind passionate about both sound and visuals

My name is Varrel Aditya Pratama Avianto, also known as NRVA. I'm a multi-disciplinary artist based in Indonesia, specializing in music production and graphic design.

My journey began with drum training at age 6, which evolved into electronic music production and sound design. Parallel to this, I discovered my love for visual arts, specializing in digital illustration and brand identity design.
