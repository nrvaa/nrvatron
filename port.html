About Me

A creative professional with a passion for both music and visual arts

I'm a multi-disciplinary artist based in Bandung, Indonesia, blending the worlds of music and graphic design to create unique sensory experiences. With over 5 years of experience in both fields, I've developed a distinctive style that combines emotive soundscapes with striking visual aesthetics.

My music journey began with a drum training at age 6, evolving into electronic music production and sound design. Parallel to this, I discovered my love for visual arts, specializing in digital illustration and brand identity design.

Today, I work with clients worldwide to create cohesive audio-visual experiences that resonate with audiences and leave lasting impressions.
